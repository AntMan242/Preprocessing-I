Work History

The Killing Joke, May/1988

After a failed career as a comedian, I began my life of crime as the masked villain Red Hood, which led to me falling into a liquid of chemicals which bleached my skin, and turned my hair green. I took my criminal activity to a new level and shot and paralyzed the one they call Batgirl. I also kidnapped and torchered Gotham's police commisioner James Gordon.

A Death in the Family, November/1988

I escaped from Arkham Asylum prison, kidnapped a Doctor who's son was the new Robin, beat him with a crowbar and tied him up next to his mother inside of a warehouse, and blew up the warehouse like it was the 4th of July. Ask batman if they survived [hehe]. This was some of my best work because it devastated ol Bats.

The Joker's Last Laugh, October/2001

I found out I was going to die of a brain tumor and i needed some very fulfilling work before I was no more. I used a venomt to "jokerize" all the inmates at Slab Penitentiary. The venom bleached their skin and turned their hair green, and they looked just like me. I started a legendary riot at Slab Penitentiary, which prompted President Luthor to declare war on little ol me. As with most of my work, the casualty count was high, which brought a huge smile to my face.
